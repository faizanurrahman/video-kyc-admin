#Kaekapakae
CEDA offers loans to individual entrepreneurs with innovative business ideas or existing businesses. These loans serve various purposes such as working capital, equipment purchase, and business expansion. Individuals can access financial support to help realize their business goals and contribute to economic growth. CEDA provides financial assistance to companies seeking to expand their operations, introduce new products or services, and enhance their competitiveness. This support plays a vital role in enabling companies to grow, create employment opportunities, and make positive contributions to the local economy. CEDA supports groups of individuals collaborating on business ventures. These groups can be cooperatives, associations, or partnerships working together for joint business initiatives. Financial funding is available to empower group efforts, foster entrepreneurship, and drive economic development. The Mabogo-dinku loan is a specialized initiative by CEDA designed to back agricultural and agri-business projects. This loan type aims to bolster food security, promote rural development, and encourage sustainable agricultural practices. Through this support, CEDA contributes to both economic and agricultural advancement.
